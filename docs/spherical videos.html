what is a spherical video?

a video every direction recorded at the same time
you need specific cameras (either one camera with multilple lenses or a bundle of cameras)
processing the 360 video is called stitching (you put together the separate images into one) (we probably won't need to deal with this anymore with specific cameras)

what's the point?

you can choose what to look at, and therefore you have some sort of agency, and a more global, generalized understanding of "a place".
there is a different concept of editing, it's not so much linear as parallel. it is about carefully choosing and showing the relationships between different parts of the space.
as such, it becomes closer to set design / scenography than to time-based editing.

software

importing into Unity:
1- import the picture
2a- change the texture setting in the inspector as texture shape > cube
2b - change the texture setting in the inspector as mapping > latitude - longitude layout
3 - create new material
4 - set the new material shader to skybox > cubemap 5- Window > rendering > lighting settings > skybox material and set it to your previsouly created material
